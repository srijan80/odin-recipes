Noodles





1. **Chicken Noodle Soup:** Ingredients: Egg noodles, shredded cooked chicken, chicken broth, sliced carrots, sliced celery, minced garlic, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Instructions: Cook noodles. Sauté garlic, add broth, carrots, celery, thyme, chicken. Simmer till veggies tender. Add noodles, season, and serve.

2. **Garlic Butter Shrimp Noodles:** Ingredients: Spaghetti, shrimp, butter, minced garlic, red pepper flakes (optional), lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Instructions: Cook pasta. Sauté garlic, shrimp, red pepper. Add lemon juice, salt, pepper. Toss with pasta. Garnish and serve.

3. **Vegetable Stir-Fry Noodles:** Ingredients: Udon or soba noodles, mixed veggies, vegetable oil, soy sauce, oyster sauce (optional), hoisin sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, green onions, sesame seeds (optional). Instructions: Cook noodles. Stir-fry garlic, ginger, veggies. Add sauce. Toss with noodles. Garnish with green onions, sesame seeds.

Enjoy these quick and tasty noodle recipes!
