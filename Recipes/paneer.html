Noodles




Ingredients:

250g paneer, cubed
2 tbsp ghee or butter
1 large onion, finely chopped
2-3 tomatoes, pureed
1/2 cup fresh cream
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1 tsp garam masala
Salt to taste
1/2 cup water
Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:
a. Heat ghee or butter in a pan, add chopped onions, and saut√© until golden brown.
b. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Then add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
c. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
d. Pour in water and let the gravy simmer for a few minutes.
e. Add paneer cubes and fresh cream. Mix gently to coat the paneer with the gravy.
f. Cook for another 2-3 minutes on low heat.
g. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with naan or rice.
