Momos





Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
Mixed vegetables (cabbage, carrot, bell peppers, etc.)
1 small onion
2-3 cloves of garlic
1-inch piece of ginger
2 tablespoons cooking oil
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon vinegar
Salt and pepper

Instructions:
Make a dough using flour, salt, and water. Rest it for 15-20 minutes. Saut√© garlic, ginger, and onions in oil. Add mixed vegetables and cook. Season with soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Roll out small dough balls into thin wrappers. Place filling in the center of each wrapper. Fold and pleat the momos to seal. Steam for 8-10 minutes until cooked. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
Enjoy your vegetable momos!
